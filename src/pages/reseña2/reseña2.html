B.A.W.

June 9, 2016

"What Do You Mean" has that tropical house feel as does "Sorry" (though he is being sued for a snippet in this now). "Company" is pretty hand clappy catchy. "The Feeling" the duet with Halsey leans a little alt-pop and "Children" goes David Guetta like house. Overall, it didn't make me want to pull it out of the disc drive and throw it across the room--yes I got the cd as it was on sale and cheaper than the MP3 at the time so why not. While I'm not saying you should log on and get this now, because every purchase does continue to fund his grating immaturity, but it is worth listening to.
